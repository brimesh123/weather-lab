# Privacy Policy for Weather Spinner

**Last Updated: November 27, 2024**

## Introduction

Weather Spinner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Weather Spinner is a completely offline educational game. The app:
- Does NOT collect any personal data
- Does NOT require account creation
- Does NOT track your location
- Does NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
- Does NOT share any information with third parties

## Data Storage

All game progress (discovered elements, levels, coins) is stored **locally on your device only**. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to any server
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Internet Permission

The app requests INTERNET permission solely for the WebView component that displays the game interface. This permission is required by the Flutter framework but **no actual internet connection is used or required** to play the game.

## Children's Privacy

Weather Spinner is designed for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
